# To use offline functionality

### To see the offline page, disconnect from Internet
* You should see the following page in your browser 
<img width="1430" alt="Screen Shot 2022-11-08 at 5 21 48 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/89402649/200688562-60b42c95-9aaa-40dd-9662-f976e78e0dc1.png">
* Type your name and your client's name into the corresponding text boxes
* Press clock in when you are ready to clock in to your shift
<img width="1412" alt="Screen Shot 2022-11-08 at 5 27 54 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/89402649/200689663-2c43b2a4-917e-4529-bb6d-558217a6a87b.png">
* Press clock out when you are ready to clock out of your shift 
   * After pressing the clock out button, a file will be saved on your device with the following information: your name, your client's name, clock in time, and clock out time. To see this file, navigate to your device's downloads folder. 
   * on computer
<img width="352" alt="Screen Shot 2022-11-08 at 5 37 31 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/89402649/200691279-a69554af-af8d-48de-a6bb-01fee8875751.png">
   * on mobile device navigate to your files app (looks like the following on iphone)
<img width="630" alt="Screen Shot 2022-11-08 at 5 44 02 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/89402649/200692090-b3c34471-0c68-4bda-a773-191fdfb56541.png">
* Once you have navigated to your downloads folder you should see a file labeled "offline.csv"
<img width="802" alt="Screen Shot 2022-11-08 at 5 49 09 PM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/89402649/200692899-3ac99798-f71e-4c9f-b2df-26535efb25eb.png">
* Open this file to view your clock in and clock out data
